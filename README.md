PART 1 — PROJECT OVERVIEW
1. Website Choice
Business Name
Novamobile
Business Description
Novamobile is a South African technology retail business offering new and pre-owned smartphones and laptops at affordable prices. The business targets customers with strict budgets who still require reliable, quality technology.
Reasons for Choosing this Business
This business was chosen due to a personal interest in technology and a desire to address the lack of affordable mobile devices and laptops in South Africa. Smartphones are among the most desired consumer items in the country yet remain expensive for many. Laptops have similarly become essential for students and professionals. Novamobile aims to bridge this gap by providing accessible technology for everyone.
Website Goals and Objectives
•	Reach a wider market through an online presence.
•	Provide customers with a convenient way to browse and purchase products.
•	Showcase products and services for marketing and promotional purposes.
•	Build business credibility through online customer reviews.
•	Deliver affordable tech while maintaining quality service and value.

5. Website Pages and Content
The Novamobile website consists of the following pages. Each page follows a consistent white-and-grey colour scheme with shared navigation, header, and footer components.

Page	Description	Key Elements
Home	Landing page; displays the brand, featured products, and quick navigation. Includes an image carousel/slideshow.	Logo, slogan, carousel, nav bar, product highlights, contact details, footer links
Products	Merged page for new and pre-owned phones and laptops in a CSS grid layout with product names and prices.	Logo, nav, product grid (phones/laptops, new/pre-owned), prices, footer
Accessories	Lists all accessories (smartwatches, earbuds, headphones, cases, hard drives) with prices.	Logo, nav, CSS grid, item names, prices, footer
Track Order	Allows customers to track their delivery by order number. Includes Express Delivery info.	Logo, nav, order search input, courier number, footer
About	Business background, vision, mission, team photo, and embedded Terms & Conditions.	Logo, nav, About Us, Vision, Mission, Team photo, T&C, footer
Contact	Enquiry form and repairs & returns information combined on one page.	Logo, nav, contact form (name, email, message), return policy, support details, footer
